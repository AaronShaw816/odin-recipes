Protein Waffles

These high protein waffles are made with cottage cheese, oat flour and eggs. So good youâ€™ll never make them any other way!

Breakfast can be a tricky meal to get healthy with so many tasty recipes out there. With these waffles, you will be getting 17 grams of protein with 4 waffles. To get even more protein, you can top yours with nut butter plus banana's and strawberries.

I know seeing cottage cheese in the ingredients may spook you, but trust me, you wont even be able to taste it! You may want to make so many of them, good news you can just freeze the left over ones and pop them in the toaster for a quick breakfast!

Ingredients

  • Cottage Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener
  • Oat Flour
  • Baking powder and kosher salt

Steps

  1. Heat waffle iron on it's medium setting.
  2. Place cottage cheese, egg yolks, vanilla, and sugar, if using in the blender. Add 6 tablespoons water, oat flour, baking powder and salt and blend until mixture is smooth batter. Transfer to a medium bowl using a spatula to get all the batter out.
  3. Beat the egg white to soft peaks then fold into the batter.
  4. Spray pre-heated waffle iron with oil spray. Pour batter into iron (about 1/4 cup) and cook until golden brown and steam is no longer being released.
  5. Top waffle with your desired toppings and enjoy!
